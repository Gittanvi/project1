About

Bhainsdehi is a town and a nagar panchayat in Betul district in the state of Madhya Pradesh, India. . Bhainsdehi holds the position of being the originating place of the river Purna. It is predominantly a tribal Tehsil. The town is surrounded by the mountain ranges of Satpura. Kukru is the second highest point of Mahadeo hills which is situated near Bhainsdehi. Its a beautiful town to visit because it is surrounded by nature. Except from these you will also see a lot of agriculture land here. It is located 46 KM towards South from District head quarters Betul.
